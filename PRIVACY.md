Who changed grades

Who edited attendance

Backups

Automated daily backups

Encrypted

Stored in a separate region

Periodic restore testing

Why: Human error causes more data loss than hacking.

Versioning & Change History

Grade history (what changed, when, by whom)

Attendance edits tracked

Why: Prevents accidental or malicious tampering

Monitoring & alerts

Graceful handling of outages
